About The Engine

Lightweight

SypherEngine is lightweight, coming in at just below 10MB, ensuring that there's no bloat in your game.

Ease of Use

SypherEngine is easy to get started in, and learn. With an abundance of docs, with examples also on the way, you'll never get lost.

Cross Platform

SypherEngine supports buiding games for Linux, MacOS and Windows. Targeting multiple platforms becomes a breeze with CI and jpackage
